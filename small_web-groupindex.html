Small web

This section contains articles on technologies that assist in retaining a personal, non-commercial corner of the Internet. The terms "small web", "smolweb", and "IndieWeb" all denote the use of ordinary web protocols and formats (HTTP, HTML), but in specific ways. Gemini is a relatively new, very simple protocol that uses specific servers and browsers, running alongside the regular web.
